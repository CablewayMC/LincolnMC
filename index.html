About Me

I'm Lincoln, a 15-year-old developer from The Hague, Netherlands. Got my first PC at 10 and went straight into coding — C# first, then Java, Python, and now C.

I've coded plugins and scripts for 10–20 live Minecraft servers, built custom clients, done hardware security work with ESP32s, and explored deep technical Minecraft mechanics like bedrock breaking.

I'm finishing middelbare school (kader) and starting Software Development at ROC Mondriaan in Delft. Long term I'm aiming for HBO — Software Engineering or Cyber Security.

I tried flipping a 2008 Intel laptop into a home server. It did not survive. That's just how it goes sometimes.
